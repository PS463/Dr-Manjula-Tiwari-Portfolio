About Dr. Manjula Tiwari

Dr. Manjula Tiwari is a passionate academician dedicated to environmental education and sustainable development. She has extensive experience in teaching Environmental Science (EVS) and has a deep commitment to student learning.

She has taught Environmental Science to BTech first-year students and is appreciated for making complex environmental topics easy to understand and engaging. Her teaching style is student-friendly and she encourages awareness about sustainability.

  • Ph.D. in Environmental Studies (Topic: Groundwater Contamination & Health Impact in Western UP)
  • Qualified UGC-NET in Environmental Science (2012 & 2015)
  • M.Sc. in Environmental Science with distinction from CCS University Meerut
  • B.Sc. in Zoology, Botany, Chemistry from CCS University Meerut
  • Diploma in Yoga from Gurukul Kangri University, Haridwar
  • Certified in Human Values & Professional Ethics from AKTU Lucknow
